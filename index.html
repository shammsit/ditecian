FOOD TO BE AVOIDED

  1. Whole milk and its products like Ghee, Cream, Butter, Cheese, Sandesh, Rasogolla.
  2. Ice-cream, Chocolate, Cakes, Pastries, Cookies.
  3. Carbonated beverages like Thumps up, Limca, Citra.
  4. Alcohol.
  5. Nutritious beverages like Horlicks, Viva, Complan, Bournvita, Maltova, Nutramul, Boost, Sperti.
  6. Dried fruits and nuts like Raisins, Pista, Almond, Cashew nut, and Pea-nut (ground nut), Coconut and its products.
  7. Fried snacks i.e. Paratha, Luchi (Puri), Samosa (Singara), Kachuri, Chop, Cutlet.
  8. Sweets of all kinds i.e. Sugar, Jaggary, Honey, Jam, Jelly, Marmaleds, Sugar-candy.
  9. Mutton or any other red-meats, Egg-yolk, Prawn, Lobsters, Shrimp.
  10. Vegetables Potato, Sweet Potato, Arvi (Kochu), & (Zimikand).
  11. Fruits Banana, Mango, Jack fruits.
