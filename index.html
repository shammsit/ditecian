FOOD TO BE AVOIDED

  1. Whole milk and products like Ghee, Cream, etc.
  2. Ice-cream, Chocolate, Cakes, Pastries, Cookies.
  3. Carbonated beverages (Thums Up, Limca, etc.).
  4. Alcohol.
  5. Nutritious beverages (Horlicks, Complan, etc.).
  6. Dried fruits and nuts (Cashew, Almond, etc.).
  7. Fried snacks (Paratha, Puri, etc.).
  8. All sweets (Sugar, Jaggery, Honey, etc.).
  9. Red meats, Egg yolk, Prawn, etc.
  10. Potato, Sweet Potato, Arvi, etc.
  11. Banana, Mango, Jackfruit.
